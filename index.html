Seunghoon Jeong

I am currently a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at the RPM Robotics lab, Seoul National University, advised by Prof. Ayoung Kim.

My research interests focus primarily on 3D reconstruction techniques such as 3D Gaussian Splatting, their applications in robotic manipulation, and the artificial intelligence methodologies that support these tasks.
